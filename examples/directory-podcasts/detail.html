About the show

The Memory Palace is a podcast about history — but not the kind you learned in school. Host Nate DiMeo finds the forgotten corners of the past: the small moments, the overlooked people, the stories that fell through the cracks of time.

Each episode is a tiny, self-contained story — usually under 15 minutes — told with the care and craft of a short story. DiMeo's writing is poetic and evocative, turning historical footnotes into something that feels both ancient and achingly present.

Perfect for: History lovers who want something different. People who like their podcasts short and sweet. Anyone who appreciates beautiful storytelling.

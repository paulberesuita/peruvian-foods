About the Roaster

Counter Culture Coffee was founded in Durham, North Carolina in 1995 by Brett Smith. From the beginning, the focus was on sourcing exceptional coffees directly from farmers â€” a practice that wasn't common at the time.

They were among the first roasters to publish detailed transparency reports, sharing exactly what they pay farmers versus the commodity market price. This commitment to openness has made them a leader in ethical sourcing.

Their roasting philosophy emphasizes clarity and balance. They aim to highlight the inherent characteristics of each coffee rather than imposing a house style. The result: coffees that taste distinctly of their origin.

About

Sey Coffee opened in 2017 with a singular focus: exceptional coffee, nothing more. The founders, Tobin Polk and Lance Schnorenberg, wanted to strip away everything unnecessary and let the beans speak for themselves.

The space reflects this philosophy. Concrete floors, minimal seating, a clean bar. No wifi, no outlets — just you and your coffee. It's a place designed for tasting, not lingering.

They roast their own beans on-site and source directly from farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Central America. The menu changes seasonally, and the baristas are happy to guide you through the current offerings.

What to know

Single-origin focus

All beans are single-origin and roasted in-house.

No wifi

Intentionally disconnected. Bring a book instead.

Limited seating

A few stools at the bar. Most people take their coffee to go.

Beans available

Take home a bag of their current roast.
